---
title: "What is Relio?"
description:
  "Relio is the first comprehensive solution for managing and ensuring the integrety of your APIs from source code to runtime."
---


Your users – be it other teams working with your private APIs or end customers using your public APIs – all depend on one crucial aspect: the API fulfilling its promises. This commitment to ensuring that APIs perform as expected is what we call **API Integrity**.

However, managing this aspect of APIs can be a complex and time-consuming task, requiring constant vigilance against breaking changes, misaligned implementations, and documentation drift.

Relio's platform streamlines the process from source code to runtime, ensuring the integrity and consistency of your APIs.Relio empowers your team to confidently manage APIs, allowing you to focus on delivering outstanding API experiences.
